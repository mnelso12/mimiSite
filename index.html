Meet mimi - the best app for live audio streaming.

Live Audio

Calls are broadcast live, with virtually no delay.

Private Channels

Every call has a private channel. Only those with the unique Call Number can listen.

Unlimited Listeners

No matter how big or small, your audience will hear you loud and clear.

The Problem

AV sytems haven't improved in nearly four decades.

Hardware is clunky and tempermental. Connections are bad. Channels are limited.

Our Solution

We make audio streaming free and simple.

Your device. Your channel. Your time.

How it Works



Speaker

Listeners

1. Press "Speak"

2. Share Call Number

3. Press "Listen"

. . . it's that simple!

Is it free?

Of course! No one should have to pay to be heard.

Are calls private?

Yes! Calls are only available to listeners who know the call number.
